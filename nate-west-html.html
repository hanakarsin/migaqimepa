Nate West - Age, Family, Bio

Drummer and cinematographer who shoots videos for YouTube star Niki Demartino of Niki and Gabi. He gained attention when Niki announced the two were dating in a YouTube vlog. 

Before Fame

He is a graduate of Temple University. 

Trivia

He is a fan of Blink-182. His Instagram, where he is known as thenatewest, has over 90,000 followers. 

Family Life

He and his mother are Philadelphia Eagles fans. He started dating Niki Demartino in April 2017. In June 2022, Niki posted an update that they remain friends but are no longer together. 

Associated With

He did a drum cover of Halsey's song "Roman Holiday" for his YouTube channel on May 25, 2017.
