MC Calls Out Makeup Artistes, Decorators for Eating at Weddings: Youre Not Part of Budget

- Some people have a bad habit of gate-crashing weddings and eating food meant for guests, leaving the newlyweds in debts

- Service providers such as photographers, makeup artistes and decorators are the main culprits

- According to Mc King of Radio, Wesley, service providers should leave once they are done with their job

There is nothing as annoying as inviting a specific number of people to a party or wedding but end up with more guests exceeding your budget.

It gets beyond your control, and in most cases, your special guests end up not eating or even fail to secure a place to sit and stand throughout the event.

This happens, especially if some of the guests come with extra uninvited people, service providers hanging around even after doing their job.

A popular Ugandan radio presenter and Mc King of Radio, Wesley, has called out decorators and makeup artistes who eat at weddings after providing their services.

"Dear service providers, this is for you, especially decorators. You come to an event to decorate with a group of 10 people, and you eat a lot of food and drink like you were invited. You were paid to decorate, not decorate, eat, and drink even though some of the invited guests miss food because of you. Makeup artistes also, after their work, they stay and eat," he wrote in part.

He added:

"The person brings the cake then stays and eat the food. I am not trying to be mean, but you were not budgeted for, those caterers bring food for only the plates paid for. The only service provider to eat the food is the Emcee."

His sentiments ignited mixed relations among his followers and below are their comments:

Ritah Martha Elvis wrote:

"You have forgotten the photographers."

Travis Sean Gosipboy:

"Please state that when you're booking us, that you won't provide food or transport so that we can add the money on the charges and see if you won't pay it. In fact we will charge you double for the food."

Bayega Jonathan said:

"Mc arrives at the reception that's around midday. He shouldn't eat as well. Also, it is only the photographers that should eat among the service providers."

Mercy Momo wrote:

"Also, musicians shouldn't eat at weddings. I once attended a wedding where an artiste was performing, and his whole crew ate first, and many guests missed food."

"So decorators come the night before, and they are not supposed to eat? An Mc comes at midday, and he’s supposed to eat? Is it because you are an Mc? As a decorator, I usually ask the clients if they will cater for our meals. If they can’t, then we sort ourselves."

Moustey Mustapha wrote:

"If you don't budget for the sound crew and Dj, I don't accept to work with you. Such impunity."

Enock Mwebe said:

"Even Mcs are not supposed to eat. They come to talk, not talk and eat."
