Singer Kefee Is Out Of Coma

Popular Nigerian singer, Kefee is reportedly out of coma.

Singer Kefee

Though family members are yet to confirm the report, sources close to the 'kokoroko' singer say she is awake and asking after her family members and friends.

The Sapele born gospel singer had collapsed on a flight going to Chicago, forcing the plane to make an emergency landing.

She was rushed to the hospital where and has been in coma for weeks now.

On June 4, Kefee's family officially confirmed the news that she was in coma, with her husband begging Nigerians to pray for his wife.

According to report, Kefee is 6-months pregnant and was diagnosed with pre-eclampsia, i.e. pregnancy induced high blood pressure.

A Nigerian medic Dr Adeyefa, however revealed that Kefee's life could be in serious danger, saying if the foetus had been 9 months, it could have been removed, which would save both lives, of the mother and the baby.
