Foreign Affairs CAS Ababu Namwamba assures William Ruto of support in 2022

- Ababu Namwamba said Ruto was the best bet for Kenya in 2022

- The former ODM loyalist vowed to walk with the deputy president in the next General Election

- He expressed hope President Uhuru Kenyatta will honour his word and stick with the DP

Heavy political rhetoric dominated the annual Isambo Beach Festivals held at Che's bay in Budalangi constituency on Sunday, December 22.

Speaker after speaker, led by Foreign Affairs Cabinet Administrative Secretary (CAS) Ababu Namwamba affirmed their unwavering support for Deputy President William Ruto.

Ababu, who until 2017 was the area MP on an ODM party ticket, said he was not afraid to walk the journey with Ruto to 2022 and beyond alluding to pre-election pact that birthed UhuRuto.

"Everyone here is with you. We are together with you (Ruto) and we shall be behind you man to man to the very end. You should remember we began the journey in 2017 albeit late," he said.

The erstwhile ODM secretary general who fell out with the Raila Odinga-led outfit in 2016, expressed hope President Uhuru Kenyatta will honour his word and stick with the DP.

"When we supported you in 2017, we supported UhuRuto, we knew you would take over from the president to serve for the remaining next 10 years. That position has not changed," he assured.

Uhuru and his deputy are believed to have entered into an agreement that guaranteed both of them would stick with each other for 20 years.

Though its details remain a highly guarded secret, close confidants of the Jubilee Party hinted that Uhuru would two terms after which he hands over the presidency to the number two.

However, there have been some disquiet and silent murmurs of possible betrayal within the ruling party owing to the newfound bromance between Uhuru and Raila.

In the meantime, Ruto continues with his nationwide meet-the-people trips disguised as development tours.
