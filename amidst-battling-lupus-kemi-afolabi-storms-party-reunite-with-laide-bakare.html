Kemi Afolabi Storms Party, Reunite With Laide Bakare After Fighting Over 'Sugar Boy'

Nollywood actress who is currently battling lupus Kemi Afolabi has reunited with her former best friend Laide Bakare at a recent event in Lagos.

This is coming to barely eight months after the duo threw away their shame and fought dirty online over a younger lover, Dauda Sulaimon, more popularly known as Gida.

Kemi and Gida have been an item for a long time and she is reportedly always on edge whenever any actress tries to get close to him.

However, while working together on the set of ‘Family’, a movie produced by Usman Michael, Laide Bakare became close to Gida.

Kemi Afolabi started suspecting foul play over the closeness between Gida and Laide Bakare which crashed their friendship as they called out each other on social media.

Since then, Kemi Afolabi and Laide Bakare are rarely seen together at parties or commenting on each other’s posts online.

Laide Bakare shocked some of her fans earlier today when she took to her Instagram page to share photos of herself and Kemi Afolabi at a party as they strike different poses for the camera.
