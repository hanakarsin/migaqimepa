Wyatt Shears - Age, Family, Bio

Famous as one-half of the California punk band The Garden, he also pursued a modeling career, appearing in a 2013 Yves Saint Laurent fashion campaign. In addition, he launched a solo music project called Enjoy.

Before Fame

He and his twin brother, Fletcher, established The Garden when they were just seventeen years old.

Trivia

He and his brother have earned over 140,000 Instagram followers and more than 25,000 Facebook likes.

Family Life

The Shears twins were born and raised in Orange, California.

Associated With

He and Romez Ramirez are both punk rock singers from California.
