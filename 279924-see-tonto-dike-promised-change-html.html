Tonto Dikeh Vows To Start Tweeting In Clear Written English

Controversial actress and singer, Tonto Charity Dikeh, popularly known as Tonto Dike, has revealed a part of her that she is ready to change now, due to popular complain.

The Rivers state born actress, who came into limelight in 2005 after she became the first runner up on a reality TV show called 'The Next Movie Star' and has since featured in over 50 movies, took to twitter a few hours ago to reveal that she has decided to start writing in good English.

It is an open secret that the actress, who recently said she does not mind using pills to stay young, writes in a very awkward manner. This has always been addressed by many of her fans who feels a graduate should not be writing the way and manner she does. Her twitter and instagram posts reveal so much about how well a Nigerian actress can put English words together.
