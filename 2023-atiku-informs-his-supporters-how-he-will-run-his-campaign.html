Atiku Informs His Supporters How He Will Run His Campaign

The presidential candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP), Atiku Abubakar, on Monday revealed the type of campaign he will run ahead of the 2023 general elections.

According to Atiku, he will not run a campaign where he would insult people, but state his unity agenda.

The former vice president said the 2023 race will be between a leadership who has come prepared and the one that wants to continue the current disastrous reality.

Atiku said, “Or, some might even say, it’s a choice between fact and fiction. Proven PDP leadership vs APC broken promises.”

He further stated that he and his team are prepared to assume leadership of the country, adding that other candidates can not come close.

Atiku in a letter to his supporters appreciated them for making his campaign the best ahead of the 2023 elections.

The former vice president added, “But the race is just starting and our goal is to maintain our leading position.

“You and I are not in this campaign to make comments that would be a slur on other people. We are here to promote our Unity-SEED policy agenda.”

Atiku noted that his agenda is to unify the country and maintain the security of lives and property of Nigerians.

The PDP presidential candidate added that he has the intention to promote sound economic policies that will lift Nigerians out of poverty to prosperity; promoting a sustainable educational environment and making the people take ownership of government.

He added that he is bent on fulfilling his covenant With Nigerians.
