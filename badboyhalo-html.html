BadBoyHalo - Age, Family, Bio

Minecraft YouTuber who has garnered more than 3.6 million subscribers for his channel. He has also posted a number of videos about banning hackers, random servers, MunchyMC.com and more.

Before Fame

He launched his channel in May of 2012 with a username of thesaintsofgames.

Trivia

The first video of his to reach the 1 million views mark was called "MINECRAFT: THE LAST UPDATE."

Family Life

His real name is Darryl Noveschosch. He has brothers and a sister.

Associated With

He is often the subject of trolling of Skeppy and returns the favor in his own videos.
