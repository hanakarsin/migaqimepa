Carmie Sellitto - Age, Family, Bio

Also known online as touchdalight, he is a video content creator who makes sarcastic and random videos for his subscribers' enjoyment. In November 2017, he joined the web group The Social Climbers.

Before Fame

He joined the YouTube world on January 29, 2012, publishing his first video called "Don't Watch This" 3 days later.

Trivia

His YouTube channel has accumulated more than 650,000 subscribers.

Family Life

He is from Hampshire, England and has two sisters. He began dating fellow YouTuber Kate Elisabeth in 2018. They split in late 2021. They have appeared on each other's channels, but they have since split. In October of 2022 he posted a video to his Instagram about taking a long-lost-love that he had met on Bumble years ago on a series of dates. 

Associated With

He cites YouTubers like Jana Vlogs and Joe Sugg as some of his favorites.
