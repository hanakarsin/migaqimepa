Shanelle Workman - Age, Family, Bio

Actress who starred as Sarah "Flash" Roberts on the soap opera One Life to Live. She has also done voice work for the video game Dino Crisis 3, Crash Tag Team Racing, and Splatterhouse.

Before Fame

As a child, she appeared in a Broadway production of Les Miserables.

Trivia

She voiced Wendy in North American commercials for the popular fast food chain Wendy's.

Family Life

She married actor David Barry Gray in 2007, and they have two children.

Associated With

In 2012, she became temporary guardian of her younger sister, actress Ariel Winter, following allegations of Ariel's abuse by their mother.
