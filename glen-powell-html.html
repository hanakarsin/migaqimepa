Glen Powell (TV Actor) - Age, Family, Bio

Series regular on Fox series Scream Queens who has also been seen in Sex Ed and The Expendables 3. In 2016, he played Troy in Ride Along 2 and starred in the Netflix original movie Set It Up in 2018. In 2019, he was cast in Top Gun: Maverick starring Tom Cruise.

Before Fame

He was featured in a minor role in the 2003 film Spy Kids 3-D: Game Over. Before his first year of college at the University of Texas, he landed a role in the film The Great Debaters as Preston Whittington.

Trivia

He can be seen in a minor role in the third installment of Christopher Nolan's Dark Knight franchise.

Family Life

Born in Austin, Texas, he moved to Los Angeles, California for his career in entertainment. He was in a relationship with Nina Dobrev in 2017. He began dating Gigi Paris in 2020, but they broke up in 2023. 

Associated With

He landed a role in the Richard Linklater-directed film Everybody Wants Some (2016).
