Kevin Bojorquez - Age, Family, Bio

Primarily known on the app TikTok, he posts lip sync videos featuring himself in a range of different costumes, often wearing wigs. He has a large following on Instagram, where he creates video content in addition to simply posting pictures, and he was also a popular live broadcaster on YouNow. 

Before Fame

The first thing he ever did on Twitter was retweet SelfieC's tweet "@TheJusticeC wth I miss you so much."

Trivia

Several other social stars such as Danielle Cohn and Tyler Brown have appeared in pictures on his kaiabojorquez Instagram account. He has promoted for popular beauty companies like Tula and Tash Cosmetics on his Instagram. 

Family Life

He is from Las Vegas, Nevada. He has an older sister named Giselle Henriquez, whom is also popular on the web. He also has a younger sister named Allison. 

Associated With

He posted a picture with fellow social stars Jacob Sartorius and Baby Ariel at the Radio Disney Music Awards in 2016.
