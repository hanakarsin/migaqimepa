Terrence J - Age, Family, Bio

Also known as Terrence Jenkins, he had roles in the 2010 film Burlesque and the 2012 film Think Like a Man. After hosting 106 & Park from 2006 to 2012, he became the co-anchor of E! News.

Before Fame

He was elected Student Government Association President at North Carolina A&T State University.

Trivia

He hosted Are You the One? in 2017.

Family Life

He dated Nicole Bilderback from 2008 to 2010. He later began dating Jasmine Sanders.

Associated With

He acted alongside Jordin Sparks in the musical film Sparkle.
