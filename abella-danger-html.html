Abella Danger - Age, Family, Bio

Named the 2016 AVN and XBIZ Best New Starlet, she is an adult film performer who first entered the industry in July 2014.

Before Fame

She was a ballet dancer from the age of three.

Trivia

The translation of her stage name is "a beautiful danger."

Family Life

She is of Jewish and Ukranian descent. She moved to Los Angeles, California after starting her adult film career in Florida.

Associated With

She has worked with Phoenix Marie.
