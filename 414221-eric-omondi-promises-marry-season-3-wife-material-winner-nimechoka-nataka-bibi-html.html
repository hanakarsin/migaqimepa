Erico, as he is famously known by most Kenyans on Wednesday, June 3, disclosed to fans that he will actually marry the winner of his reality show, Wife Material, for real.

In a long Instagram post seen by TUKO.co.ke, Eric said what started as a joke has become a real thing and that he will walk down the aisle with whoever wins in his reality TV show.

''Hey guys. I hope you had a lovely day. Every journey has its ups and downs. Season one of #WifeMaterial was almost PERFECT!!! Season two was a TOTAL DISASTER...Sometimes I sit back and ask myself what was the intention of this show, what was the original idea???...And then I remember the MAIN REASON. I was actually really looking for a wife to marry, settle with and start a family, then things happened and that's life, but the best thing about life is that it offers us the opportunity to REFOCUS, to remember why we do what we do and why we began whatever we began in the first place,'' Eric wrote on IG.

He went on to promise his followers that he will be honest this time round and honour his word by marrying the winner of his reality show.

According to the comedian's post, he is currently tired and wants to get his better half, start a family and enjoy his happily ever after with his wife and later, his children.

Eric confirmed season three of his show will premiere soon and that it will feature 'candidates' from countries such as Kenya, Rwanda, Ethiopia, South Sudan and Nigeria.

The comedian noted that he will be turning 40 in about nine months, therefore, needs to settle down.
