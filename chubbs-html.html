Chubbs - Age, Family, Bio

British YouTuber who discusses the latest goings on in the world of YouTube personalities on his popular channel, Chubbs. He is perhaps best known for his series "If...Was Honest."

Before Fame

He grew up in England.

Trivia

He has a gaming channel called ChubbsXL. His YouTube video "Jseized - Who am I? (Animated Music Video)" has over 6,500 views. Collectively, he has seen the videos on his channel achieve over 200,000 total views. He is known to occasionally post audio and music videos on his channel. 

Family Life

His real name is Billy Bradfield. He has three siblings.

Associated With

He has criticized YouTubers like Keemstar as part of his "If...Was Honest."
