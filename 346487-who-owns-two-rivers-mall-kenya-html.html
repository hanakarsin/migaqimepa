Who owns Two Rivers mall in Kenya?

If you are looking for beautiful sceneries to visit and have fun, Two Rivers Mall is without a doubt the best place to go. It is a fun-filled place that has all the necessary amenities, attractions, and amusements. Not to mention, there is no entrance fee. But do you know who owns Two Rivers Mall?

Two Rivers Mall is located in Ruaka in Nairobi, Kenya. It was opened on 14th February 2017. The mall is in close proximity to the affluent neighborhoods of Gigiri, Runda, Muthaiga, and Nyari. Within two weeks after being opened, around 10,000 people would visit this Mall every day to enjoy the most entertaining activities and sceneries the mall has to offer.

Two Rivers is known to be the largest mall in East Africa. It provides extraordinary entertainment, which is not found in other malls. This mall is opened every day starting from 9 a.m to 9 p.m

Who owns Two Rivers Mall?

The mall is owned by Two Rivers Lifestyle Company Limited, which has two shareholders namely: Two Rivers Development Limited and OMP Africa Investment Company. Each of these shareholders holds a 50 per cent stake in the mall.

Two Rivers Development Limited has three shareholders namely: Centum Investment (58%), AVIC International (39%), and ICDC (3%). On the other hand, OMP Africa Investment Company is a group company of Old Mutual Group.

The owner of Centum Investment is Chris Kirubi. So, does Chris Kirubi own Two Rivers Mall? He may as well be declared the owner of this mall since he is the owner of Centum Investment, which is the largest shareholder at Two Rivers Development Limited.

The investment by Avic is particularly noteworthy being one of the largest foreign direct investments in this region by a Chinese corporation into a private enterprise.

This mall is part of the Kshs 25.1 billion mixed-use projects being undertaken by Two Rivers Lifestyle Company on a parcel of land measuring 102 acres. This piece of land was bought from the Koinange family. Originally, the mall was to cover 1.2 million square feet of land. However, this was increased to 1.7 million square feet in a bid to increase the size of lettable space from 460,000 square feet to more than 700,000 square feet.

So how much did it cost to build Two Rivers Mall? Overall, the estimated construction cost is about Kshs. 15.5 billion. The apartment cost was around Kshs 3.8 billion, while the infrastructure development was Kshs 5.8 billion. Phase two of the Kshs 25 billion-project is scheduled to begin soon.

Is Two Rivers Mall a secure place to visit?

Most people may have a fear of visiting the mall due to the terrorist attacks by the Al-Shabab that happened at the Westgate Shopping Mall. You should have no more worries about your security when visiting the mall. The Managing Director of Two Rivers Mall has done everything possible to guarantee the security of visitors of the mall.

They have installed over 300 security cameras. Also, they have hired security operators who monitor every single activity, which is carried out in every corner of the mall.

They also have security guards who not only provide security but also keep an eye on every kind of suspicious activity that can be noticed in the mall. For example, if you leave a bag lying down for more than 10 minutes, the security surveillance team of Two Rivers Mall will view it as a potential threat; hence action will be taken immediately.

How prepared is Two Rivers Mall to handle a fire incident?

As we all know, malls are public places, and they always have the risk of catching fire. The fire outbreak can cause loss to both human lives and goods. The owners of Two Rivers Mall have installed adequate fire extinguishers and fire watch security guards who maintain fire security in the mall. They have also hired a group of Red Cross attendants who give first aid and handle medical emergencies which can occur in the mall premises.
