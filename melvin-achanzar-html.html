Melvin Achanzar - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube personality and computer hacker who rose to fame as a member of the Project Zorgo group. Since leaving, he has amassed more than 3.3 million subscribers on his PZ9 The Best Fighter channel. He has also posted gaming videos about Among Us and Roblox on his channel. 

Before Fame

In July of 2019, he published a video titled "Hey Chad Wild Clay! It's me PZ9 Your Best Friend Hacker! Can You Hear Me Down Here?"

Trivia

His music video, "Come Back Chad" has been viewed in excess of 61 million times in total. He is a member of the Spy Ninjas content group. He also plays guitar and has posted content of himself singing and playing on his spyninjamelvin Instagram. 

Family Life

He and his sister, Regina, were born and raised in Nevada. They are of Filipino-American ancestry.  He is also known as Melvin Genera.

Associated With

He has collaborated with fellow content creators Vy Qwaint and Chad Wild Clay.
