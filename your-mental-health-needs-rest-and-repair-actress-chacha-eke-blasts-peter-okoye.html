'Your Mental Health Needs Rest And Repair'

Nollywood actress, ChaCha Eke, has slammed Nigerian singer, Peter Okoye, over his randy moment with a female fan on stage.

Naija News earlier reported that the married singer, whilst performing alongside his brother, Paul Okoye aka Rudeboy at a recently sold-out concert, passionately kissed a yet-to-be-identified female fan amongst a cheering crowd.

Some fans screamed in excitement with the lucky fan gasping for breath momentarily.

Mr P then went back to the stage and joined his brother, the other half of Psquare to continue the performance.

This earned him criticism online as many Nigerians tackled him for disrespecting his wife, Lola Omotayo Okoye.

Peter Reacts

Amidst the backslash, the singer in a tweet shared on his page averred that as an artist, he can throw his clothes, and shoes including kisses.

He noted that nobody from his family is complaining about the issue, hence, questioning why people are pained.

The singer urged critics to allow them to continue their music tour in peace.

ChaCha Eke Slams Singer

Reacting to this, ChaCha in a video shared on her Instagram page lashed out at the singer, questioning his mental health.

According to the actress, who had once revealed she is suffering from bipolar, Peter Okoye’s mental health needs rest and repair.

She captioned the video: “Your Mental Health Needs Rest & Repair, Dear “Mr.P. Take Time To Eat & Sleep.”
