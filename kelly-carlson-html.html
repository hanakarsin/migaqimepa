Kelly Carlson - Age, Family, Bio

Portrayed Kimber Henry on the FX drama series Nip/Tuck in what is perhaps her most well known role. She has also appeared on the big screen in Paparazzi and The Marine. 

Before Fame

She played a motorcycle gang member in the film 3000 Miles to Graceland and worked as a model early in her career.

Trivia

She has posed for Maxim magazine and appeared on the cover of Stuff. Maxim also named her the 43rd hottest woman of 2005.

Family Life

She was born in Minneapolis, Minnesota and attended the Academy of Holy Angels.

Associated With

She made a guest appearance on the William Petersen starring series CSI: Crime Scene Investigation.
