Chris Ballinger - Age, Family, Bio

Magician who hosts Magic Mondays on his YouTube channel Ballinger Family, formerly called BallingersPresent, in addition to family vlogs. He has released DVDs by the names of The Shark and Monarch. 

Before Fame

He created his YouTube channel on January 7, 2013. He has since accumulated more than 1.6 million subscribers.

Trivia

He features a segment on his channel called Bailey's Bookshelf. He has a large golden doodle dog named Rue. 

Family Life

He has featured both of his sisters, Rachel and Colleen Ballinger, in several of his videos. He also has a brother named Trent. He married Jessica Ballinger and he has kids named Bailey, Jacob, Parker, Duncan, Luke and Teddy.

Associated With

One of his more popular videos is titled "Jennifer Lawrence Magic Trick." In September 2014, he collaborated with JoshuaDTV on the video "Improv Duet Challenge with Joshua Evans."
