Deevon Osley - Age, Family, Bio

Widely known for his VonVonTV YouTube channel's content. He has gained popularity there for his pranks, reaction videos, original comedic sketches, challenge completions, and personal Story Times. He has frequently impersonated other people in his pranks in order to test the loyalty of those around him.  

Before Fame

He began his YouTube channel on January 24, 2014. 

Trivia

He has amassed more than 450,000 subscribers to his VonVonTV YouTube channel. One of his most popular videos is from his Catfish series. It has over 3 million views. He has a secondary YouTube channel called VonVon Reacts and he also broadcasts live on YouNow. 

Family Life

He was born and raised in Pontiac, Michigan. His entire family is active on YouTube, including his brothers Young Monte Carlo and Silly T.O. In 2020, he became a father to his daughter, Nyomie.

Associated With

He collaborated with fellow YouTube star PontiacMadeDDG on his YouTube video "1 VS 1 Against PontiacMadeDDG | Epic Battle!!"
