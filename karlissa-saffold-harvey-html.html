Karlissa Saffold Harvey - Age, Family, Bio

Best recognized for being the mother of rapper Blueface. She is also the CEO of the series CIXOT THE REAL LA FAMILY BLUES SEASON. Her Instagram account, which operates under the handle karlissa_angelic, has garnered 260,000 followers. 

Before Fame

She graduated from Warren G. Harding High School. She studied at the College of the Canyons.   

Trivia

She has worked as a weight-loss coach at My Angelic Fitness.   

Family Life

She is married to Sterling Saffold. In addition to Blueface, she has a son named Olscokcy and a daughter named Kali.

Associated With

Her granddaughter is Jaidyn Alexis.
