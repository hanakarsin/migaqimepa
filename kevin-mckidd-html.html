Kevin McKidd - Age, Family, Bio

Scottish actor best known for his role as Dr. Owen Hunt on the long-running television series Grey's Anatomy. He also appeared on the series Rome from 2005 to 2007. 

Before Fame

He started his acting career with the comedy group The Improverts. 

Trivia

His film credits include Percy Jackson & the Olympians: The Lightning Thief, Trainspotting and Kingdom of Heaven.

Family Life

He married Jane Parker in 1999. The couple had two children, Joseph and Iona, before divorcing in 2016. He then married Arielle Goldrath, who he was with for five years. They share son Aiden and daughter Nava together. They later divorced. In June 2023, he began dating Danielle Savre. 

Associated With

He acted alongside Patrick Dempsey in TV's Grey's Anatomy and the romantic comedy Made of Honor (2008).
