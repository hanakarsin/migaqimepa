Jack Bandeira - Age, Family, Bio

Film and television actor best known for his role as Crow in the 2021 action film Gunpowder Milkshake. He is also known for his appearances in Sex Education, Venom: Let There Be Carnage and The Duke. In 2022, he was selected to star in the drama film My Policeman as well as the Netflix action series Lockwood & Co. 

Before Fame

He officially began his acting career in 2013 while on the show Middleton. He only made one appearance on the show. 

Trivia

He also appeared in series like Holby City, The Witcher, Young Wallander and Vera. In 2022, he played Dale in the video game As Dusk Falls.

Family Life

Growing up, he lived with his family in London, England. 

Associated With

He co-starred alongside Lena Headey and Angela Bassett in the 2021 action film Gunpowder Milkshake.
