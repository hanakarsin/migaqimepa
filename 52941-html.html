Don Jazzy Clocks 31 Today

One of Nigeria's Multi award-winning record producer, singer-songwriter, and CEO of Mavin Records Label, Michael Collins Ajereh popularly known as Don Jazzy clocks 31 today, 26 November, 2013.

Don Jazzy was born in Umuahia, Abia State on November 26, 1982. He lived and was raised in Ajegunle, Lagos state.

A Business Management Graduate from the Ambrose Alli University Ekpoma, Edo state he moved to London in 2000.

Don Jazzy who is one of the popular producers in Nigeria was rated as the 36th "Most powerful celebrity in Africa" according to Forbes. He had always had a passion for singing and playing drums from the age of four.

The former CEO of Mo'Hits record label first signing was his former label mate who was the Vice-President before their split, D-banj and his full credit production work was in 2004,  Dban'j's Tongolo.
