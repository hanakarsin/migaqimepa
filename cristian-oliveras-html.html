Cristian Oliveras - Age, Family, Bio

Social star best known for his comedic skits and lip syncs where he often impersonates a number of goofy characters. He posts the videos to his Instagram account, which has over 800,000 followers, as well as to his YouTube channel. In 2018, he began starring in the Brat series Turnt. 

Before Fame

He created his self-titled YouNow account in July of 2013, but had previously been active on Twitter as JustMeChristian in December of 2012. He previously had a different Twitter account, but it was suspended. 

Trivia

He was cast on season nine of MTV's Wild 'N Out, becoming the first openly gay cast member on the show. He has earned over 4 million followers through his comedic videos on his cristianoliveras TikTok account. 

Family Life

He and his brother are from Orlando, Florida.

Associated With

He tagged Lele Pons in his Vine video "When you see the price on something u like."
