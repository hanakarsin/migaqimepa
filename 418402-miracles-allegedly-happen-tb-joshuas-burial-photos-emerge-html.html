Miracles Allegedly Happen at TB Joshua's Burial, Photos Emerge

  • The burial ceremony of TB Joshua was reportedly more of a deliverance service for many members of his church
  • Miracles and supernatural experiences allegedly happened on July 9, 2021, after the interment
  • Some church members allegedly received healing when a video of Joshua praying was played

Even in death, the spiritual power of Prophet TB Joshua, the late leader of the Synagogue Church Of All Nations (SCAON), is supposedly evident.

A Facebook post from the TB Joshua Ministries claimed miracles and deliverance took place immediately after his interment on Friday.

The organisation said supernatural experiences started when a video clip of Prophet Joshua's prayers was played.

TUKO.co.ke reported that a fire broke out on July 5 when members of Joshua’s church were attending his funeral service.
