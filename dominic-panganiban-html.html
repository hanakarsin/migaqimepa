Domics - Age, Family, Bio

Flash animator best known for his comedic videos on his popular YouTube channel, which features content ranging from anecdotes to social satires. He has exceeded over 7 million subscribers. One of his most viewed videos, "Crushes", has received over 31 million views since it was posted in September of 2016. It featured him reflecting on romantic experiences in grade school. 

Before Fame

He first created his YouTube channel on August 11th, 2012.

Trivia

He has an archive of comics on his personal tumblr blog. CBS News and Malay Mail Online have featured his work. His twitch has featured multiple videos of him playing the game Step Mania. 

Family Life

His full name is Dominic Panganiban. He was born in Manila, Philippines. He started dating a cosplayer known as Chikarabbit in mid-2016. He was previously in a relationship with K1tana.

Associated With

He has featured Jack Douglass on his YouTube channel.
