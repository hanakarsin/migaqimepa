An associate of a popular Islamic cleric, Sheikh Ahmad Gumi, Mallam Tukur Mamu has highlighted reasons why he withdrew from negotiation with terrorists that attacked the Abuja-Kaduna bound train in March 2022. Speaking during an interview with Arise TV on Thursday, Mamu, who has been playing the role of a mediator between the government, the terrorists and their victims, said that he will no longer participate in dialogue with terrorists as his life is in danger.
