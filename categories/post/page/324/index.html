Arsenal Set To Appoint Arteta As New Manager

English Premier League side, Arsenal, have decided to appoint Manchester City’s assistant coach, Mikel Arteta as the club’s new manager. A further report had it that the chief executive of the North London side, Vinai Venkatesham and chief contract negotiator, Huss Fahmy, were spotted leaving Arteta’s house in the North West London in the early hours of Monday.
