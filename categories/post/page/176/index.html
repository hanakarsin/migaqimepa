Sanwo-Olu Signs Bill Regulating Real Estate Transactions In Lagos

published on May 01, 2024
Governor Babajide Sanwo-Olu of Lagos State on Monday signed a bill regulating real estate transactions in the state. Naija News reports that Sanwo-Olu signed the bill at the Executive Chamber at the Lagos House in the Ikeja area in the presence of the Commissioner for Housing, Moruf Akinderu-Fatai; the Attorney General and Commissioner for Justice, Moyosore Onigbanjo; the governor’s Special Adviser on Housing, Toke Awoyinka-Benson; amongst others. Speaking at the signing, the Governor said the law was created to regulate the real estate transactions in Lagos State will help to protect the citizens; the unsuspecting Lagosians who fall prey to many real estate professionals.
