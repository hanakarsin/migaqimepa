Don Panton - Age, Family, Bio

Widely known for comprising one half of the Panton's Squad YouTube channel. He has earned widespread popularity there for his couples' vlogging, including pranks and challenges, alongside his wife Malinda Panton.

Before Fame

He and his wife first met in 2006. They dated for a year before marrying.

Trivia

One of his most popular YouTube entries, "Panton Squad Official Music Video "We Go Hard"" has earned over 7 million views. It features the Panton Squad performing a song about their family. 

Family Life

He and his wife Malinda have children together named Sariyah, known as Yaya, and DJ. In May 2021, they welcomed their third child, a son named Bryson.

Associated With

He and Joseph Robinson are both known for their couples' pranking on their respective YouTube channels.
