Anjini Dhawan - Age, Family, Bio

Instagram sensation who's famously known as the niece of the Bollywood actor Varun Dhawan. She has gained 260,000 followers on her self-titled Instagram account. 

Before Fame

She posted her first photo on Instagram in June 2016. 

Trivia

She's friends with model Aaliyah Kashyap. Many of her Instagram posts feature her golden retriever. It also features several fashion and workout posts.

Family Life

Her father is the actor Siddharth Dhawan. Her grandparents are Anil and Rashmi Dhawan. 

Associated With

She frequently posts photos on Instagram with Khushi Kapoor.
