published on June 19, 2024
The crisis rocking the All Progressive Congress (APC), yesterday took a new twist as a youth wing of the party dumped it for the ruling Peoples Democratic Party (PDP). The defecting youths cited the hijacking of the party’s structure by the recently defected PDP governors as the reason for the mass exodus. The leadership of APC-National Youth Frontier in a press conference in Abuja said what they have witnessed so far in the party was the direct opposite of their expectation and aspiration.
