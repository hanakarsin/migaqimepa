APGA Candidate, Soludo Defeats Ozigbo, Andy Uba In Their LGA

The Governorship candidate of the All Progressives Grand Alliance, Charles Soludo has secured the highest votes in Aguata Local Government Area. Valentine Ozigbo, the candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party, Andy Uba of the All Progressives Congress and Soludo are all from Aguata. The Collation Officer for Aguata, Prof. Anim Ajake while announcing result at the Local Government Collation Centre, said Soludo polled 9,136 votes, while Uba had 4773 votes and Ozigbo trailed with 3798.
