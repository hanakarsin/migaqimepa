PDP Wins First Polling Unit In Osun Election

published on August 19, 2024
The Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) and its governorship candidate in Osun State, Ademola Adeleke has won its first polling unit in the ongoing governorship election in the state. officials of the Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC) declared the result of the polling unit in Osun State, on Saturday. Senator Adeleke of the PDP gathered 47 votes at Polling Unit 05, Ward 07 in Ilesa West Local Government Area, while the state Governor and the candidate of the All Progressives Congress, Gboyega Oyetola polled 17 votes.
