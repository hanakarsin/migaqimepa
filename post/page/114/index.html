Senate To Screen President Tinubu's Ministerial Nominees On Monday

published on June 08, 2024
The Senate is breaking its own rules to start the screening of President Bola Tinubu‘s 28 ministerial nominees on Monday, July 31. Under normal circumstances, the Senate does not conduct business on Mondays. The Chairman of the Senate Committee on Media and Public Affairs, Senator Yemi Adaramodu explained this unexpected move while speaking with journalists after a plenary session. He emphasized that each nominee would undergo rigorous screening. “In light of the significance of this national assignment, we’ve suspended all our rules to commence the exercise next Monday,” Adaramodu declared.
