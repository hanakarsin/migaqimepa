We Are Ready To 'Stop' Bandits, Kidnappers, Others

published on April 01, 2024
The Miyetti Allah Cattle Breeders Association of Nigeria (MACBAN), has said it is ready to cooperate with the government to tackle the ongoing insecurity challenges in the country. The herders union acknowledge that kidnappers, bandits and cattle rustlers have put the nation in a bad state and there is a need to take decisive actions to stop the perpetrators. MACBAN in a statement issued in Abuja on Sunday, noted that it has developed a strategy to curb the menace so that pastoralists can resume their activities and locals can regain their territories which had been occupied by the deadly groups, Naija News reports.
