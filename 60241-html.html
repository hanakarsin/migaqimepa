UNBELIEVABLE: SEE How Woman Survives Cement Truck Crash On Her Car (PHOTO)

A 39 year old Chinese woman, Lok Wan, had a miraculous escape after a truck overturned and spilled a full load of wet cement on her car.

 *Rescuers at the scene of the accident

Rescuer pulled the woman out of the wreckage after the cement crushed her car in Fuzhou, Fujian Province.Amazingly, she was almost unscathed, apart from being covered in cement, after her 40 minute ordeal.

The crash happened after Wan and his girlfriend Yin Min Tai, 35, took the wrong entrance to a busy motorway and decided to make a U-turn.

A cement-laden lorry travelling close behind the couple was unable to stop in time and ploughed into the back of their car, shedding its load all over the vehicle.

Wan was able to scramble free of the car but rescuers initially thought there was little chance of Yin Min Tai surviving.

As they began clearing the vehicle of cement, however, they heard her moaning and 40 minutes later were able to pull her free.

Remarkably Yin Min Tai was left with little more than a few cuts and bruises as a result of the accident.
