Skyler Gisondo - Age, Family, Bio

American actor whose earlier credits include The Amazing Spider-Man, Night at the Museum: Secret of the Tomb, and Vacation. He began starring as Eric on Netflix's Santa Clarita Diet in 2017. In 2018, he'd star in Time Freak as Evan.

Before Fame

He began acting in small television roles at the age of 7.

Trivia

He had a recurring role as Young Shawn in USA's Psych. 

Family Life

His parents, Ron and Stacey Gisondo, are engineers. 

Associated With

He played the brother of Emma Stone's character in the film The Amazing Spiderman.
