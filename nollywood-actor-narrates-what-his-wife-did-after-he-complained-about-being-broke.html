Nollywood Actor Narrates What His Wife Did After He Complained About Being Broke

Nigerian actor and producer, popular known for his roles in Yankee Hustle, Deyemi Okanlawon has revealed how his wife came to his rescue over a recent financial dilemma.

Okanlawon, via his verified Twitter handle on Monday, opened up about the kind gesture he received from his wife.

According to the father of two, he summoned the courage to tell his wife that he was low on cash, and rather than make a big deal from the revelation, his wife credited his account beyond his demands.

Sharing his wife’s gesture, he wrote: ”Last night I finally told babe how low my cash flow was and how I still had plenty pending commitments…. na so I wake up this morning see credit alert wey pass my problem.”

Deyemi Okanlawon Opens Up About Strange Illness 

Meanwhile, Deyemi recently recounted how he endured excruciating pain for 2 years.

According to Deyemi, who underwent a successful surgery, he had to work in spite the pain he felt, adding that he had to smile through it with very strong pain meds. The actor also thanked his wife and colleagues for the support and care showered on him during those times.

Sharing a photo, he wrote; ”For almost 2 years after an unsuccessful medical procedure I’ve had to live through the most excruciating pain imaginable… at night I literally screamed and cried, by day I worked and smiled through it all (with really strong pain meds). Post-surgery I feel like I’ve been given a new lease on life. I want to say a special thank you to my wife for the love and care, to my fam & friends for their prayers and support, to my many brand partners and employers for their patience and understanding, and to the best surgeon ever, Dr Coker. His entire medical team at Lagoon Hospital I say a big thank you. Grateful to God for the road to divine recovery!”
