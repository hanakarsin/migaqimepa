If you did not know Koffi Olomide's age, then you would be surprised to learn that the Congolese singer is 64 years old, though nothing in his life gives the impression that he's approaching the later stages of his career.

The musician continues to create hits while showing off an incredible fashion sense for a grandpa, and TUKO.co.ke shines a light on some of his unique and crazy looks.

1. The singer was born Antoine Christophe Agbepa Mumba back in 1956 and started music as a background vocalist in the 1970s.
