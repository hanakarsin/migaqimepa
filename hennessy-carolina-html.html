Hennessy Carolina - Age, Family, Bio

Social media star who built her following as the younger sister of Love & Hip Hop: New York star Cardi B. She has earned over 7 million followers on Instagram. She has pursued a career in fashion and styling. 

Before Fame

In March 2015, she began to build her own social media following by posting videos, selfies, and memes to her Instagram account.

Trivia

In September 2016, she starred in a YouTube music video for SwiftOnDemand. In November 2020, she posted a reel where she set up a fishbowl for her new fish. 

Family Life

Her parents were born in the Caribbean, though she and her sister were raised in the Bronx.

Associated With

Her sister stars on Love & Hip Hop: New York alongside Tahiry Jose.
