Miya Horcher - Age, Family, Bio

Actress best known for playing Cassandra Evans in the 2020 film Switched. She has appeared in series including The Rookie and School of Rock.

Before Fame

She made her screen debut in a 2014 episode of The Middle.

Trivia

She voiced several characters in the video game Fallout 4.

Family Life

Her sisters are actresses Madalyn Horcher and Madison Horcher.

Associated With

Her costars in Switched included Madeleine Byrne and Denise Richards.
