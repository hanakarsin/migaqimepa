PDP Wins First Polling Unit In Osun Election

The Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) and its governorship candidate in Osun State, Ademola Adeleke has won its first polling unit in the ongoing governorship election in the state.

officials of the Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC) declared the result of the polling unit in Osun State, on Saturday.

Senator Adeleke of the PDP gathered 47 votes at Polling Unit 05, Ward 07 in Ilesa West Local Government Area, while the state Governor and the candidate of the All Progressives Congress, Gboyega Oyetola polled 17 votes.

A total of 120 voters registered in the polling unit while 71 were accredited for the ongoing governorship election.

The total number of valid votes was 67 while four votes were voided.

This is coming after Davido on Saturday claimed that the news coming in across Osun State in the ongoing governorship election favours the PDP.

The singer called on voters in the state to be vigilant, claiming that the election was rigged around this period in the 2018 governorship.

Davido charged electorates who have not voted to come out and vote for the governorship candidate of PDP, Ademola Adeleke who happens to be his uncle.

The singer further stated that more vote is needed as the voting period is closed to ending.
