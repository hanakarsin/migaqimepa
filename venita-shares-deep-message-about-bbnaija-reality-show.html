Hermes: 'You Should Have Won All The Prize Money'

Former Big Brother Naija Housemate, Venita Akpofure has faulted the eviction of ‘Level Up’ housemate, Hermes.

Naija News recalls that three of the season 7 housemates Groovy, Sheggz, and Hermes were evicted on September 25, during the Sunday live eviction.

However, Hermes’ eviction came as a big shock to many of the viewers who had thought the reality TV star will make it to the top 6 finalists or possibly win the N100M grand prize.

Reacting to the eviction, Venita Akpofure, in a post shared on her Instagram page reiterates that Hermes would have won the prize money.

Sharing a deep message and dynamics of the game, Venita said the reality show experience never favours the pure-hearted.

Notwithstanding, the ex-housemate wished Hermes success in his future endeavours adding the real deal is outside the biggie house.

She wrote: “You should have won all the prize money in my honest opinion. But this experience never favours the pure-hearted! Nonetheless, the real tea is outside the house! Shine bright baby!”

Davido Pays DJs To Play Only His Songs

Meanwhile, American-based Ghanaian singer and promoter, Mr Music Mensah has said that Davido often pay DJs at clubs in the United States to play his songs.

The singer made the claim during an interview with Nana Romeo on Accra Fm as he recounted an incident he witnessed, DklassGH reports.

Mensah said he was at a nightclub when he saw the singer buying out the club’s patronage.

The “Blessing” singer,m further revealed that Davido paid for everybody’s food and drink, and paid the Dj to play only his songs.
