Ariel Fulmer - Age, Family, Bio

Founder of Fig + Stone Designs, an online shop that specializes in vintage and handmade items. She is a designer and curator with expertise in vintage decor, textiles, furniture and art. 

Before Fame

She attended The American School in London, Universite Sorbonee Nouvelle and Carleton College, the latter of which she earned a degree in sociology and anthropology. 

Trivia

After taking years of chemistry courses and apprenticeships in art conservation, she elected to enter the corporate world and became a buyer for a large online decor retailer. 

Family Life

She married Ned Fulmer of BuzzFeed's The Try Guys in June of 2012. They welcomed their first child, Wesley James, in 2018. Their second child, a son named Finn, was born in 2020.

Associated With

Her husband Ned was a member of The Try Guys with Eugene Lee Yang and Zach Kornfeld.
