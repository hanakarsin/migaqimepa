Walter Miller - Age, Family, Bio

Published fashion and art photographer perhaps more well known for being a cast member of VH1's Black Ink Crew. He became a series regular in season 6 after spending the first five seasons as a recurring player.

Before Fame

He worked as an assistant manager for Black Ink 113th.

Trivia

His da_wizrd Instagram account featuring his personal and professional life earned more than 340,000 followers.

Family Life

He is from Harlem, New York.

Associated With

He and Young Bae both became regulars on Black Ink Crew in season 6.
