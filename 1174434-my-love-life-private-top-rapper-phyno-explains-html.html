My love life is private to me - Top rapper Phyno explains

- Phyno has spoken on the kind of woman he wants

- The popular rapper explained that he doesn’t like lousy women

- He also explained that he likes his private life to remain private and not become public news

Popular rapper Phyno recently spoke about the kind of women he likes. He also explained that he doesn’t like details of his private life becoming public news.

An entertainer who rarely shows off his possessions online, Phyno, also hasn’t been dogged with baby mama drama like some of his colleagues.

“On the surface, I like a girl who is beautiful. But I cannot stand a lousy girl. I like someone who is industrious and can think for herself. For me, it is more about the person’s character and not the physical qualities; that can fade away at any time.

My love life is private to me and it shouldn’t be a topic for public discussion. As a celebrity, there are a few things about me that are not out there already, but I still strive to keep certain things private. I am not one to bare it all on social media,” he told Punch.

He further explained how he gets inspiration for his songs: “The funny thing is that I get inspiration from a variety of things and places. I am basically inspired by everything.”
