Mya - Age, Family, Bio

Pop and R&B singer known for her contribution to the cover of "Lady Marmalade" with Christina Aguilera, P!nk, and Lil' Kim. Her single "My Love is like...Wo" became Gold-certified.

She was an exceptional tap dancer and performed at the Kennedy Center. One of her tap groups was called T.W.A., or Tappers With Attitude.
