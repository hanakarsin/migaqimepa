Adetoun Threatens To Beat Up Kemi Olunloyo

Media personality, Adetoun has confronted controversial Journalists Kemi Olunloyo for allegedly cooking up stories against her.

Recall that Naija News earlier reported that Femi Falana had called on the Lagos State Police to invite Kemi Olunloyo for questioning over her statement that 12-year-old Sylvester Oromoni agreed to be beaten up by his seniors in other for him to join a cult.

This is coming after Nigerian actress Ada Ameh also threatened to beat Kemi Olunloyo over her allegations against the deceased boy.

In another twist, Adetoun who was known for her active participation in the Endsars protest in 2020 had taken to her Instagram account to post a video of her calling Olunloyo and promising to beat her up.

Adetoun also called her out for peddling lies against her, Iyabo Ojo, Davido, Tiwa Savage and others.

She wrote: “KEMI U GO CHOP BEATING.
WICKED WOMAN FROM A PIT OF HELL.
THANK GOD U SPILL THE MILK URSELF.

“ME I DONT TALK MUCH OOO
NA U GO OPEN UR YANSH URSELF. STUPID INGRATE.

“IF U NEED GENUINE STORY. GO TO CHANNELS TV THEY WILL TELL U ALL THAG HAPPENED. @CHANNELSTELEVISION.”
