Ava Hinojosa - Age, Family, Bio

Short-form comedy and lip-sync content creator on TikTok who rose to fame on her stackinbarbie account. She has more than 950,000 followers on the platform.

Before Fame

She made her TikTok debut in April of 2020. She began her career as a content creator by surprising strangers with her low voice.

Trivia

In February of 2021, she posted a TikTok that went viral, receiving more than 1 million views in under a month. 

Family Life

She was born in Sacramento, CA.

Associated With

In late 2020, she created a TikTok set to Whoheem's "Let's Link."
