Serena Williams, Adorable Alexis Stun the Net with Amazing Snaps

- Tennis champion Serena Williams recently shared some of the photos she took with her beautiful daughter Alexis Olympia Ohanian

- In the pictures, the mother-daughter duo could be seen on a tennis court dressed up in stunning gowns and armed with tennis rackets

- Many of the star's fans complimented the two on how beautiful they looked and how they were having a good time taking photos together

Serena Williams and her daughter, Alexis Olympia, are near to many people's hearts. This is why social media was positively lit up after the tennis star shared stunning shots of herself having fun with her little one on a tennis court.

The adorable mommy-daughter duo dressed up in exquisite gowns while armed with tennis rackets, and the two pretended to be playing a tennis match.

The best part of the photos, however, was how happy they looked spending time together.
