Regina Daniels Replies Those Mocking Her For Marrying 'Grandpa'

Nollywood actress and billionaire’s wife, Regina Daniels has slammed persons making a jest of her marriage on social media.

Naija News reports that the mother of two made the revelation in response to those mocking her for marrying Nigerian politician and businessman, Ned Nwoko, who is 61-year -old.

Recall the actress traditional tied the knot with her aged husband in April 2019, and had come under heavy criticism due to the 38-year age gap between the couple.

Taking to her Instagram, the mother of two asked critics to “shut the f**k up.”

Regina had earlier shed more light on why she married Ned Nwoko as his 6th wife.

“I don’t think I could have married someone of my age because I am quite stubborn, very stubborn, my head is not down but with my husband that is not the case because I respect him a lot,” she noted.
