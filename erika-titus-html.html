Erika Titus - Age, Family, Bio

TikTok star known for her outgoing personality and for posting quirky comedy videos. She also tells stories from her life on her self-titled account, where she has earned more than 2.4 million followers. 

Before Fame

She posted her first TikTok in November 2019. 

Trivia

In May 2021, she posted a TikTok of her skincare routine. She has posted multiple beach photos on her erikamtitus Instagram account, where she has accumulated over 210,000 followers.

Family Life

In May 2021, she shared a Mother's Day Instagram post, writing "mother still won't allow a mom reveal." Her older sister Kirsten frequently appears on her TikTok account. Her older brother's name is Jacob.

Associated With

In August 2019, she shared concert videos of Daniel Caesar on her Instagram account.
