October 31 Horoscope and Zodiac

Ambitious, intelligent and understanding, your natural abilities are well-suited for numerous careers. You may find passion in helping others, which could lead to a career in philanthropy, counseling or education. Similarly, your ambition may be a good fit for the world of business, media or advertising. If the world of entertainment calls you, a career in directing or acting may be rewarding, which was the case for Peter Jackson, who was also born on October 31st. In whichever career you choose, look to be a pioneer or visionary, much like Christopher Columbus, another of your celebrity birthday twins.
