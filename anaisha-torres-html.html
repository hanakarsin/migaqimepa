Anaisha Torres - Age, Family, Bio

Instagram sensation with a following of more than 400,000 fans. She has gained her immense collection of followers by posting a mix of selfies, dance clips, and TikTok lip sync videos.

Before Fame

In April 2016, she opened her Instagram account with a photo captioned "First picture G'morning guys have a great day."

Trivia

In addition to using Instagram, she also became a popular TikTok creator, accumulating tens of thousands of followers on the lip syncing app making videos to the tune of artists like Akon, Bastille, and Justin Bieber. 

Family Life

She has two siblings, one of whom, Jacob, is also an Instagram and TikTok star.

Associated With
