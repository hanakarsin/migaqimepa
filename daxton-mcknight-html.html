Daxton McKnight - Age, Family, Bio

Adopted son of Mindy McKnight, the creator of Cute Girls Hairstyles, and her husband Shaun McKnight, who helps run the successful YouTube channel. He has appeared in the family's vlogs and routine videos. 

Before Fame

He was born in Park City, Utah and the whole family traveled to the hospital to be there for his birth. 

Trivia

He was first introduced to Cute Girls Hairstyles fans in a "Family Intro" video on September 21, 2010. He was featured in an October 2021 vlog where he was participating in a church egg drop challenge. 

Family Life

He joined a family of kids that included twins Brooklyn and Bailey, Kamri Noel and Rylan McKnight. 

Associated With

He has a younger adopted sister named Paisley McKnight.
