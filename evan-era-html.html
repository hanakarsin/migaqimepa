Evan Era - Age, Family, Bio

Magician and YouTube prankster who has amassed a following of more than 3.1 million subscribers by posting magic trick tutorials, social experiments, and pranks to his EvanEraTV YouTube channel. He also has a second channel for vlogging called EvanEraVlogs.

Before Fame

Rather than opening his channel with a prank, he debuted his YouTube channel with a video titled "Evan Era vs Jus Juice Rap Battle."

Trivia

One of his most popular YouTube videos, "3 EASY Magic Tricks - How To Magic!," has earned more than 1 million views since its February 2015 posting.

Family Life

He was born Evan Rosenman in Louisville, Kentucky. He had a daughter named Ava with his ex-wife Lauren Chavez.

Associated With

He has made appearances in a number of Chase Gilroy's videos, like "Prank Fail!" and "Ahhh Busted!"
