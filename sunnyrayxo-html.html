sunnyrayxo - Age, Family, Bio

Best known for cosplaying superheroes such as Spider-Man and the Black Widow. She also does characters from games, such as Kitana from Mortal Kombat. Her Sunnyrayyxo Instagram account features cosplay portraits, informal selfies, and lifestyle content. She has amassed 4.7 million followers.  

Before Fame

She became active on Instagram in January 2021. 

Trivia

She goes by Cyber Kitty, and many of her cosplay looks involve cats. One of her earliest Instagram photos is a cosplay of Michelle Pfeiffer's interpretation of Catwoman. Many of her TikToks on her cyberkitty.xo channel have received over 1 million views. 

Family Life

She was born in the United States. She has pet ducks and chickens.

Associated With

Libby Zaz is another popular American cosplayer. She would occasionally feature music by Doja Cat in her TikTok cosplay videos.
