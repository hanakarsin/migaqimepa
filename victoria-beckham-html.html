Victoria Beckham - Age, Family, Bio

Known as Posh Spice, Beckham is a former Spice Girl who has released several top-ten UK singles during her solo career, including "Out of Your Mind." She was joined in the Spice Girls by Geri Estelle Halliwell, Melanie Janine Brown, Emma Lee Bunton and Melanie Jayne Chisholm. 

Before Fame

Her father would drop her off at school in his Rolls Royce, and other children would throw rocks at her. She went on to join the band Persuasion.

Trivia

The Spice Girls' 1996 song "Wannabe" became a #1 hit in thirty-one countries, including the United Kingdom and the United States.

Family Life

She married English soccer star David Beckham on July 4, 1999 and they had four children together: Brooklyn, Romeo, Cruz and Harper.

Associated With

She began her modeling career in 2000 and in 2008, she was the face of Marc Jacobs' spring collection.
