Manal Benchlikha - Age, Family, Bio

Singer and songwriter who is known for having participated on the competition series Mantsayadch. She is known for also having earned representation from the Magic Castle Entertainment label. 

Before Fame

She first began earning recognition for her singing in 2015 when she released the single Denia. She then went on to release the popular hit Koulchi Ban in 2017 which earned her further recognition. 

Trivia

She is also known for her popular YouTube channel where she releases covers, live recordings, and music videos for her songs for her over 1.8 million subscribers. 

Family Life

She was born in Casablanca, Morocco but was raised in Marrakesh. 

Associated With

She is known for being a protege of DJ Van and is signed to her record label.
