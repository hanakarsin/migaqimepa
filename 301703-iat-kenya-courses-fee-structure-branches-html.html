IAT Kenya courses, fee structure, branches

IAT is an institution that is on a mission. Right from its tag line, which is, “Your Success Is Our Responsibility”, this school whose full name is Institute of Advanced Technology aims to ensure that its students are the best in the market. The hands-on and practical teaching approach has proved successful in ensuring students retain the skills they have learnt. Do you want to find out if the institution has the required accreditation to offer its courses? Here are the details.

To start with, the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Technology have mandated the Institute of Advanced Technology as a Technical & Vocational Training Authority (TIVET) centre. The mandate allows this school to train Artisans, and offer courses at the level of certificate, diploma, and higher diploma. This piece highlights all the programs that are available at this institution, its fee structure, and branches.

Certificate level

  • Certificate in Business Administration & Management (CBAM)
  • Certificate in Business Information Technology – CBIT
  • Certificate in Information Technology Management - CITM

The entry requirements for these certificate programs are an aggregate grade of D+ (D PLUS) in K.C.S.E. and a D (Plain) in Mathematics. You will need one year to complete these courses.

IAT Diploma courses

  • Diploma in Internet System and Security (DISS)
  • Diploma in ICT Management – DICTM
  • Diploma in Business Administration and Management
  • Diploma in Business & ICT – DBICT

The entry requirements for these programs vary, but the minimum grades are a C- (minus) or a C (plain) in KCSE, and a C- (minus) or a C (plain) or above in English and Mathematics. The duration for these programs is two academic years.

IAT fees structure

By the look of the number of programs that this institution offers, it is evident that you will get one that suits you. To find out the specific amount of fees that each course requires, you can contact the help desk using the contact details listed below.

Apply for a course at IAT

The application process is straightforward. You can pick a hard copy form from any of their offices or opt for the online application method. In the latter, you will have to provide your details like name, email, and phone number then proceed to choose your preferred course, mode of study, and campus.
