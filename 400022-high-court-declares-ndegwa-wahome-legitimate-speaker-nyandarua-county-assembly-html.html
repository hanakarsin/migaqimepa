High Court declares Ndegwa Wahome as legitimate speaker of Nyandarua County Assembly

- High Court Judge Joel Ngugi ordered Nyandarua Police Commander to protect Wahome as he executes his duties as the Speaker

- This comes after a section of MCAs on January 8, 2021, purported to have impeached Wahome despite a court ruling barring them

- Wahome was accused of gross misconduct and abuse of office when he moved to court to seek orders to bar the assembly from impeaching him

The High Court has declared Ndegwa Wahome as the legitimate Speaker of Nyandarua County Assembly.

In the ruling, High Court Judge Joel Ngugi ordered Nyandarua Police Commander to protect Wahome as he executes his duties as the Speaker.

The court also declared that no other person can perform the duties of the office of the Speaker which includes being Chairperson of the Nyandarua County Assembly Service Board other than Wahome.

This comes after a section of MCAs on January 8, 2021, purported to have impeached Wahome despite a court ruling that gave the Speaker seven days to respond to the dubious allegations that he was accused of.

Wahome was accused of gross misconduct and abuse of office among other ills when he moved to court to seek orders to bar the assembly from impeaching him arguing that they had not followed the right procedure.

The High Court’s ruling means the impeachment exercise carried out by less than 18 MCAs is illegal and Wahome continues to serve as the Speaker of the Nyandarua Country Assembly.

Advertisements canceled

Two advertisements that ran on Daily Nation and The Standard announcing a vacancy for the position of the Speaker of Nyandarua county were canceled.

According to the cancelation notice, the adverts announcing the position were done by an individual who is not authorized or the chairperson officer of the County Assembly Service Board.

The notice adds that the vacancy advertisement was sponsored by Nyandarua Governor Francis Kimemia whose office doesn’t have any authority to make such a transaction since the county assembly is an independent office.

Reports indicate the drama at the Nyandarua County Assembly is because of the differences between Speaker Ndegwa and Governor Francis Kimemia.

Governor Kimemia is said to be coercing MCAs to remove the Speaker saying he was an impediment to his 2022 re-election.
