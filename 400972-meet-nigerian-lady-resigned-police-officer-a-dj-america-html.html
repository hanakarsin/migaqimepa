Meet Nigerian lady who resigned as police officer to become a DJ in America

A US-based Nigerian disc jockey, Odiase Selimot Adejoke better known as DJ Sexy J, recently opened up about the unique circumstances that led to her new career path.

Adejoke in an exclusive chat with Legit TV disclosed that she was formerly an officer of the Nigeria Police Force (NPF) before she resigned to take up a job as a DJ in the US.

The entertainer disclosed she served in the police force for 15 years and went on two different peacekeeping missions.
